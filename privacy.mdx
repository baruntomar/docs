### a) Account Information

- Name

- Gender (optional)

- Social media links (optional)
- Profile picture
- User bio (optional)

### b) Content & Activity

- Posts (text, links, images, polls)

- Followed users and connections

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To create and manage user accounts.
- To facilitate discussions, posts, comments, likes, shares, and polls.
- To provide personalized recommendations (related posts, suggested connections, etc.).
- To enable co-founder matching and startup showcase features.
- To ensure platform security, enforce community guidelines, and take action against rule violations.
- To improve site functionality and user experience.
- To send notifications, updates, and marketing communications (you may opt out anytime).

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share it in the following cases:

- **Public Content:** Posts, comments, and profile details (excluding personal contact info) are visible to other users.
- **Service Providers:** Third-party services that help us operate the platform (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processors for premium features).
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to enforce our Terms of Service.
- **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred.

## 4. User Control & Choices

- **Edit or Delete Profile:** You can update or delete your account details anytime from your profile settings.
- **Privacy Settings:** Adjust what information is publicly visible.
- **Blocking & Reporting:** Users can block or report other users for violating platform rules.
- **Opt-Out:** You can opt out of marketing emails from the account settings.
