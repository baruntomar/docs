### 2. How to Add Social Links

1. Go to your \*\*Account Settings page and click on \*\*[**Socials**](https://desifounder.com/settings/socials).
2. Click on the **Add Links** section.
3. Select the relevant social icon/logo and paste the Display Text and **full URL** of your social account (example: `https://www.linkedin.com/in/yourname`).
4. Click \*\*Save \*\*button.

---

### 3. Do’s & Don’ts

**Do:**

- Link only accounts that you personally manage.
- Make sure your links are **correct and active**.
- Keep your accounts professional and aligned with community values.

**Don’t:**

- Add fake, inactive, or unrelated accounts.
- Link to offensive or harmful content.
- Share login credentials. We only require the link, never your password.
