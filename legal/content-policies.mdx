_<sub>Effective Date: January 01, 2025 • Last updated on November 05, 2025</sub>_

At **desifounder**, we aim to maintain a safe, respectful, and meaningful environment where founders, creators, and innovators can share ideas freely.

These Content Policies outline what kind of content is **allowed**, **restricted**, and **prohibited** on our platform, including the Community Feed, Spotlight, and user profiles.

By using [desifounder.com](http://desifounder.com), you agree to follow these rules.

## 1. General Principles

All content shared on desifounder should:

- Be accurate and honest to the best of your knowledge.
- Contribute constructively to the community.
- Respect others’ rights, privacy, and intellectual property.

We reserve the right to moderate, edit, or remove content that violates these principles.

## 2. Allowed Content

You can post or share:

- Original projects, products, or startup ideas.
- Helpful discussions, questions, insights, or experiences.
- Educational or industry-related materials.
- Constructive feedback or analysis.
- Light, humorous content _(within respectful limits)_.

All posts should be relevant to **startups, business, creativity, or community discussions.**

## 3. Prohibited Content

The following types of content are **not permitted** on desifounder:

### a. Inappropriate or Harmful Content

- Obscene, sexual, or pornographic material.
- Hate speech, threats, or harassment.
- Discriminatory or defamatory remarks.
- Violence, self-harm, or illegal activity promotion.

### b. Misleading or False Information

- Fake profiles, false claims, or impersonation.
- Manipulated statistics, fake testimonials, or deceptive marketing.

### c. Spam or Irrelevant Promotion

- Mass advertising, repetitive links, or unrelated promotions.
- Referral or affiliate spam unrelated to discussions.

### d. Intellectual Property Violations

- Uploading copyrighted images, videos, or text without permission.
- Using logos or trademarks you don’t own or have rights to.

### e. Confidential or Private Data

- Sharing personal data _(yours or others’)_ such as addresses, private communications, or financial info.
- Posting internal company data or trade secrets without consent, or in bad faith.

## 4. User Responsibility

- You own your content but are **fully responsible** for it.
- You must ensure that everything you post complies with these policies and applicable laws.
- Content found in violation may be deleted without notice, and accounts may be suspended or terminated.

## 5. Moderation & Enforcement

Our moderation team may:

- Remove content that violates policies.
- Issue warnings or temporary suspensions.
- Permanently ban users for repeated or severe violations.

_Moderation decisions are made at desifounder’s discretion to protect the integrity of the community._

## 6. Reporting Violations

If you come across content that violates our policies, please report it:

- Use the **Report** button on the post or profile.
- Or email us at [**contact@desifounder.com**](mailto:contact@desifounder.com)

We review every report seriously and take appropriate action.

- Users posting content are solely responsible for any legal or reputational consequences.
- desifounder shall **not be held liable** for damages, disputes, or losses arising from user content or third-party actions.

## 8. Updates to These Policies

We may update these Content Policies periodically. The “Last Updated” date will reflect the latest version. Continued use of desifounder after changes means you agree to the new policy terms.
