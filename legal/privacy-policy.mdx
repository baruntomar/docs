Welcome to [**desifounder.com**](http://desifounder.com) (“DesiFounder”, “we”, “our”, “us”).

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our platform.

We collect the following information when you register or update your profile:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number _(optional)_
- Date of Birth _(optional, private)_
- Gender _(optional, private)_
- Profile location _(optional)_
- Social media links _(optional)_
- Profile picture
- User bio _(optional)_

Certain information displayed on the Platform, including service provider listings, descriptions, and claims, is submitted directly by users. Desifounder does not independently verify the accuracy of such information.

### c) Content & Activity

We also collect data related to your actions on the platform, including:

- Posts, comments, likes, and shares
- Projects and Spotlight submissions
- Co-founder matching or community participation
- Reported or blocked users

### d) Usage Data

Includes your:

- IP address
- Device and browser type
- Pages visited and interactions with posts

### e) Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality and analyze traffic. You can adjust your browser settings to control cookies.

- Personal data is processed based on **free, informed, specific, and unambiguous consent** provided by the user.
- Users have the right to **access, correct, update, and delete** their personal data through account settings or by contacting us.

- Reasonable security safeguards are implemented to protect personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.
- For any privacy or data-related grievance, users may contact our designated grievance officer at [**legal@desifounder.com**](mailto:legal@desifounder.com).
