_<sub>Effective Date: January 01, 2025 • Last updated on November 05, 2025</sub>_

At **desifounder**, we believe in building a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative community for founders, creators, and innovators.

Our Code of Conduct ensures that every member can engage safely and meaningfully.

By using desifounder, you agree to follow this Code at all times — whether you’re posting, commenting, messaging, or submitting projects.

## 1. Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect, regardless of their background, gender, opinions, or experience level.

- Disagreements are natural, but personal attacks are not acceptable.
- Focus on ideas, not individuals.
- Use polite, professional language in all interactions.

## 2. Be Authentic

- Use your real identity whenever possible.
- Share genuine projects, experiences, and insights.
- Do not impersonate another person, brand, or organization.

## 3. Keep It Professional

desifounder is a space for entrepreneurship and collaboration.

Avoid posting or engaging in:

- Obscene, offensive, or sexually explicit content.
- Hate speech, discrimination, or harassment.
- Spam, clickbait, or unrelated promotional material.

## 4. Respect Privacy & Boundaries

- Do not share someone else’s private information without consent.
- Avoid posting confidential or proprietary company data.
- Keep direct messages professional — unsolicited or inappropriate communication is not allowed.

## 5. Contribute Meaningfully

- Share insights, experiences, and feedback that add value to the community.
- Use the right **Dock** or category when posting on the feed.
- Avoid duplicate, irrelevant, or low-effort content.

## 6. Intellectual Property

- Only upload or share materials you own or have permission to use.
- Give credit where due — acknowledge collaborators and sources.
- Plagiarism or copyright infringement will lead to account suspension.

## 7. Reporting Misconduct

If you see behavior that violates this Code:

- Use the **Report** option on the post or profile.
- Or email us directly at [**contact@desifounder.com**](mailto:contact@desifounder.com)\*\* \*\*with details.

We review all reports carefully and take appropriate action, which may include content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent ban.

## 8. Enforcement

- Violation of this Code may lead to warnings, suspension, or account deletion.
- Serious or repeated offenses (e.g., harassment, hate speech, impersonation) result in permanent removal.
- desifounder reserves the right to moderate or remove any content that breaches this Code.

## Disclaimer

- desifounder provides a platform for open discussion and collaboration but does **not endorse, control, or pre-approve** user-generated content.
- We are not liable for any user behavior, statements, or disputes arising between members.
- By participating, you acknowledge that your actions and content are solely your responsibility.

## 9. Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a welcoming environment for everyone.\
By adhering to this Code of Conduct, you help build a stronger, safer, and more inclusive community for all founders.
